FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ZenithFX.

What is ZenithFX?+
ZenithFX is a professional online trading platform that lets you trade Synthetic Indices, Forex, Cryptocurrency, Commodities and Stock Indices. It's designed specifically for African traders with M-Pesa integration, local currency support and 24/7 synthetic markets.
How do I deposit using M-Pesa?+
Go to the Deposit page, select M-Pesa, enter your Safaricom phone number and the amount in KES (minimum KES 500). You'll receive an STK push on your phone — enter your M-Pesa PIN to confirm. Funds are credited to your ZenithFX account instantly, 24/7, with zero fees. Powered by Lipana.dev.
What are Synthetic Indices?+
Synthetic Indices are simulated markets generated by a provably fair algorithm. They're available 24/7 — including weekends and holidays — and are not affected by real-world news events. They include Volatility 10-100, Boom & Crash, Step Index and Jump Index. Great for practicing and trading at any hour.
Is my money safe with ZenithFX?+
Yes. ZenithFX uses 256-bit TLS encryption for all communications and transactions. Client funds are held in segregated accounts, separate from operational funds. We require KYC verification for all live accounts and comply with AML regulations.
How quickly can I withdraw my profits?+
M-Pesa withdrawals are processed instantly — money arrives on your phone within minutes. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days. Crypto withdrawals take 30 minutes to 2 hours. All withdrawals require completed KYC Level 2. There are zero withdrawal fees.
What is the minimum deposit?+
The minimum deposit is KES 500 (approximately $3.80 USD) via M-Pesa. For card payments the minimum is $10 USD. There is no minimum for the free demo account — you start with $10,000 virtual funds immediately on signup.
Can I automate my trading?+
Yes! ZenithFX includes DBot — a visual drag-and-drop trading bot builder. No coding required. You can create strategies by combining blocks (market selection, conditions, stake, trade type, stop loss), backtest them on historical data, and run them 24/7 automatically.
