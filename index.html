FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about ZenithFX.

What is ZenithFX?+
ZenithFX is a professional online trading platform designed for African traders. Trade Synthetic Indices, Forex, Crypto, Commodities and Stock Indices. It features M-Pesa integration, 24/7 synthetic markets, DBot automation and copy trading.
How do I deposit using M-Pesa?+
Go to the Deposit page, select M-Pesa, enter your Safaricom phone number and amount (min KES 500). You'll receive an STK push on your phone — enter your PIN to confirm. Funds are credited to your ZenithFX account instantly, 24/7, with zero fees. Powered by Lipana.dev.
What are Synthetic Indices?+
Synthetic Indices are simulated markets generated by a provably fair random algorithm. Available 24/7 including weekends and holidays, they are not affected by real-world news events. They include Volatility 10–100, Boom & Crash 1000, Step Index and Jump Index.
Is my money safe?+
Yes. ZenithFX uses 256-bit TLS encryption on all communications and transactions. Client funds are held in segregated accounts, separate from operational funds. Full KYC verification and AML compliance are enforced for all live accounts.
How fast are withdrawals?+
M-Pesa withdrawals are processed instantly — money arrives on your phone within minutes. Bank transfers take 1–3 business days. Crypto withdrawals take 30 minutes to 2 hours. Zero withdrawal fees on all methods. KYC Level 2 required.
What is the minimum deposit?+
The minimum deposit is KES 500 (approximately $3.80 USD) via M-Pesa. For card payments the minimum is $10 USD. There is no minimum for the free demo account — you start with $10,000 virtual funds immediately on signup.
Can I automate my trading?+
Yes! ZenithFX includes DBot — a visual drag-and-drop strategy builder. No coding required. Create strategies by combining blocks (market, conditions, stake, trade type, stop loss), backtest on historical data, and run 24/7 automatically even when you're offline.
